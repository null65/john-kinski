About John Kinski

A writer shaped by history, theatre, and a life spent listening to people's stories.

John Kinski is a novelist with a background in history — he holds a BA and MPhil in History from the London School of Economics. His fiction spans historical comedy, contemporary thrillers, and literary suspense, united by sharp wit and a deep love of character.

When he is not writing, John works as a sports injury therapist and personal trainer in Buckinghamshire. His understanding of people — their bodies, their struggles, their secrets — informs the vivid, human characters that populate his novels.

His latest novel, Theatrecy, is a black comedy set in the 1640s during the English Civil War, following Gideon, a would-be actor on a mission to restore the Globe Theatre to its former glory.
