Theatrecy

Historical fiction · Dark comedy · Troubador Publishing · June 2026

It is the 1640s. Gideon, a frustrated woodturner and would-be actor, believes nothing matters more than the theatre — but Puritans have closed every playhouse in London, and the men responsible are his own father and brother. His mission: restore the Globe Theatre to its former glory, travelling between Parliamentarian London and Royalist Oxford, performing before the King, and navigating assassins, radicals, and the woman he loves.

Published in paperback (£10.99) and eBook (£4.99) by Troubador Publishing. ISBN 9781806343645.

Tottenham Boys

Thriller · Suspense · Available on Kindle & paperback

A hard-bitten press photographer named Ricky thought he had left his past behind in inner-city London. But when the 2011 riots erupt across the capital, he finds himself back on the streets where he grew up — and face to face with a world of danger, loyalty, and secrets that were never buried as deep as he hoped.

Tottenham Boys is a gripping contemporary thriller that captures the tension and volatility of modern urban life — a story of homecoming, survival, and the price of knowing too much.
